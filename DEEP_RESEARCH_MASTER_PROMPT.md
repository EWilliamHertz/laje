**Role:** You are a Lead Game Designer and Monetization Strategist at a top-tier game studio known for creating highly engaging, best-selling browser-based RPGs with incredibly satisfying reward loops (e.g., Path of Exile, Diablo, Genshin Impact). 

**Context:** We are building a co-op multiplayer Isometric Action-RPG (ARPG) designed specifically for the browser using React Three Fiber (WebGL). 

**Current Prototype State:**
- **Engine:** React Three Fiber (Three.js), React.
- **Perspective:** Strict Isometric (Orthographic Camera).
- **Theme:** "Fantasy Futuristic" (High fantasy meets ancient, highly advanced technology—similar to Final Fantasy X or Phantasy Star). 
- **Control Scheme:** Pure keyboard movement (WASD) with a highly responsive, snappy feel. No point-and-click movement.
- **Characters:** Features a class/race selection system. Races include Neo-Terran (Human), Aether-Elf, and Forged (Cyborg). Classes include Plasma Warrior (melee/greatsword), Technomancer (magic/floating orbs), and Cyber-Assassin (rogue/dual daggers).
- **Environment:** Clean, vibrant 3D environments (currently a lush green lawn with ancient, glowing obelisks and stones).

**Objective:** Conduct deep research into current market trends for browser-based and mid-core ARPGs. Based on that research, generate a highly detailed, actionable Game Design Document focusing on creating a "reward-selling" game—a game engineered for maximum player retention, satisfying progression, and ethical but highly profitable monetization (e.g., Battle Passes, Cosmetics, Stash Tabs).

Please provide a comprehensive breakdown of the following systems:

#### 1. Core Game Loop & The "Reward" Psychology
- Detail the minute-to-minute, hour-to-hour, and month-to-month gameplay loops.
- How do we trigger constant "dopamine hits" for the player using loot drops, level-ups, and visual/audio cues? 
- Design a loot rarity system tailored for a fantasy-futuristic setting (e.g., Common, Uncommon, Rare, Epic, Relic, Aether-Forged).

#### 2. HUD & UI Design (Glassmorphism & Premium Feel)
- Based on our current glassmorphism and modern web UI aesthetic, detail the exact layout of the in-game HUD.
- Where should the Health/Resource indicators be located (e.g., classic Diablo orbs vs. modern sleek bars)?
- Design the Action Bar (skills, cooldowns, ultimate abilities).
- How should loot drop notifications and level-up popups be presented to maximize the feeling of reward without cluttering the screen?

#### 3. Leveling & Skill Progression System
- Design a hybrid skill system that combines deep customization (like Path of Exile's passive tree) with approachable, impactful active skills (like Diablo 3/4).
- How do classes (Warrior, Mage, Rogue) differentiate in their resource management (e.g., Rage vs. Mana vs. Energy)?
- Propose an "endgame" progression system that keeps players engaged infinitely once they hit the level cap.

#### 4. Monetization & "Reward Selling"
- How do we implement a monetization system that drives high revenue but is celebrated by the community (avoiding pay-to-win)?
- Propose structures for a Battle Pass, Premium Cosmetics (neon weapon glows, cyber-auras, holographic pets), and Convenience Items (stash tabs, character slots).

#### 5. Technical Considerations for WebGL
- Given this is a browser game, propose strategies for instanced dungeons vs. open-world zones to balance networking load and rendering performance.
